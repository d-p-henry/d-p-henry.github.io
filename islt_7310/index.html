Welcome

Hello and welcome to my website!
My name is Dillon Henry. I'm a library student at the University of Missouri. I also work in a library as a Digital Curation Fellow. I spend a lot of time copying data from removable media, like CDs and floppy disks. In my studies, I care about libraries and justice. I have written about difficulties some communities have with accessing library resources. This includes poorer people, minorities, and prisoners. I'm also interested in technology and libraries. Before studying library science, I studied music. I still enjoy writing songs in my spare time. I also love reading, cooking, hiking, and traveling.

I am entering the world of professional librarianship, and the purpose of this site is to showcase my skills and experience. Here you will find: my curriculum vitae; samples of my work to illustrate my core competencies; and reflections on what I’ve done, what I’ve learned; how I’ve grown; and my goals for the future. Thank you for stopping by.
